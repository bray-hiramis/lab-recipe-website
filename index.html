Garlic Butter Chicken Recipe

Garlic Butter Chicken is a simple yet flavorful dish that combines tender, juicy chicken breasts with a rich, buttery garlic sauce. The chicken is seared to perfection, creating a crispy golden-brown crust, while the melted butter and garlic infuse it with a deliciously savory aroma. A hint of paprika and oregano adds depth to the flavor, making this dish both comforting and satisfying. Garnished with fresh parsley and served with a squeeze of lemon, this easy-to-make meal is perfect for a quick dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken: Pat the chicken breasts dry with a paper towel. Season both sides with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano.
  2. Sear the chicken: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 5 minutes per side until golden brown.
  3. Add butter & garlic: Reduce heat to medium, add butter and minced garlic to the pan. Spoon the melted butter over the chicken as it cooks for another 3-4 minutes, ensuring it is fully cooked (internal temperature should reach 165°F / 75°C).
  4. Rest & serve: Remove from heat, let the chicken rest for a couple of minutes. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve with lemon wedges.
